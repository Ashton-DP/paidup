Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

PaidUp ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA) and applicable data protection laws. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Who we are

PaidUp is an automated invoice-chasing service for South African small businesses. Our registered contact email is hello@paid-up.co.za.

2. Information we collect

  • Account data: your name, business name, and email address when you sign up.
  • Invoice data: client names, email addresses, phone numbers, invoice amounts, and due dates — either synced from Xero or entered manually.
  • Payment data: subscription plan and billing status. Card details are processed directly by Stripe and never stored by us.
  • Usage data: reminder logs, send times, and delivery status for audit and support purposes.

3. How we use your information

  • To send automated payment reminders to your clients on your behalf.
  • To manage your account, subscription, and billing.
  • To sync invoice data from your Xero account.
  • To improve the reliability and performance of the service.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-party services

We use the following sub-processors to deliver the service:

  • Xero — accounting data sync (xero.com)
  • Stripe — subscription billing (stripe.com)
  • Resend — email delivery (resend.com)
  • Twilio — WhatsApp messaging (twilio.com)
  • Google Gemini — AI message generation (ai.google.dev)
  • Railway — cloud hosting and database (railway.app)

Each sub-processor is bound by their own data protection policies.

5. Data retention

We retain your account and invoice data for as long as your account is active. If you close your account, your data is deleted within 30 days. Chase logs are retained for 12 months for audit purposes.

6. Your rights under POPIA

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to the processing of your data.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@paid-up.co.za.

7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS) for all data in transit and at rest. Passwords are hashed using scrypt and never stored in plain text. We do not store card details — all payments are handled by Stripe.

8. Cookies

We use a single session cookie to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you by email of any material changes. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@paid-up.co.za.
